Conduct a comprehensive UX audit of the UTM Builder application. Review all major screens and workflows for usability issues, visual consistency, accessibility gaps, and interaction patterns that could be improved. Document findings with specific recommendations and prioritize by impact.

Key areas to evaluate:
- Navigation and information architecture
- Form usability (link builder, presets, parameter formats)
- Empty states and onboarding flow
- Error handling and feedback patterns
- Visual consistency (spacing, typography, color usage)
- Dark mode completeness
- Mobile/responsive behavior
- Accessibility (keyboard nav, screen readers, contrast)
